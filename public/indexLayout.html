Chicken Shawarma

A delicious and flavorful Middle Eastern dish made with chicken, spices, and pita bread

Ingredients

  • "2 cups carrots, sliced",
  • "1 cup green beans, chopped",
  • "2 tablespoons olive oil",
  • "1 clove garlic, minced",
  • "1 teaspoon ginger, grated",
  • "Salt and pepper to taste",
  • "Soy sauce for flavor (optional)"


Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine the ham, pork shoulder, Swiss cheese, and pickles.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together the mustard, mayonnaise, olive oil, red wine vinegar, brown sugar, smoked paprika, garlic powder, black pepper, and salt.
  4. Spread the mustard mixture on one side of each slice of bread.
  5. Divide the ham, pork shoulder, Swiss cheese, and pickles between the bread slices.
  6. Top with the remaining bread slices.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes, or until the bread is golden brown and the cheese is melted.
  8. Let cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
